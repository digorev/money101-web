Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Money 101 is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your financial data should remain yours and yours alone.

Data Storage

All your financial data (transactions, expenses, and settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your financial information, and it never leaves your device.

Data Collection

Money 101 does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

Money 101 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please leave a review on the App Store or reach out through our support page.
